## What This Tool Does

The Science Study Planner helps CBSE Class 10 students build a structured, personalised revision schedule for all 13 chapters of the CBSE Science syllabus.

### Features

- **Chapter-wise planning** — set study dates for each of the 13 Science chapters
- **Progress tracking** — mark chapters as planned, in progress, or completed
- **Board exam countdown** — keeps the exam date visible to maintain urgency
- **Marks-weighted prioritisation** — helps allocate more time to higher-weightage units
- **Revision scheduling** — separate slots for first study, revision, and PYQ practice per chapter

## Recommended Study Strategy

**Step 1 — Concept first**
Watch the CBSE Digest video for each chapter before reading NCERT. Build understanding before memorisation.

**Step 2 — NCERT thoroughly**
Read NCERT text, answer all in-text questions, and complete all exercise questions. NCERT is the primary source for CBSE board questions.

**Step 3 — PYQ practice**
After completing a chapter, solve 5–10 years of Previous Year Questions for that chapter. Identify the exact question types that appear.

**Step 4 — Error log**
Maintain a notebook of mistakes in practice. Review and re-attempt weak areas before the exam.

**Step 5 — Revision rounds**
Plan at least two full revision rounds before the exam. Use one-shot revision videos for quick full-chapter review.

**Step 6 — Internal assessment**
Don't neglect practicals and periodic tests — 20 marks of the final score come from internal assessment.
