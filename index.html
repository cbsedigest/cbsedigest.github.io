ABOUT ME

The mind behind the method

With a strong foundation in chemistry and a focus on electrochemistry and nanomaterials, I have cultivated a diverse academic and research career.

I hold a PhD in Physical Chemistry from the University of Madras (1999), following my Postdoctoral Fellowships at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras (1998) and Sunchon National University in South Korea (2005–2007), where I worked in the Department of Chemical Engineering.

My research further expanded during a visiting scientist tenure at Jawaharlal Nehru Centre for Advanced Scientific Research (JNCASR), Bengaluru, focusing on nanomaterials and nanoelectrocatalysis in 2010.

I was also honoured to be selected as a Brainpool Research Fellow at Sunchon National University from 2014 to 2015 — one of only 32 researchers worldwide chosen by the Government of South Korea for groundbreaking research in electrochemistry.

My academic journey began with a Bachelor of Science in Chemistry from the University of Madras (1986–1989), where I graduated first in my class, and continued with a Master of Science in Applied Chemistry from Anna University (1989–1991), earning first-class distinction.

FAQS

Got questions? We've got answers

Can I attend online from Chennai, Bangalore, or anywhere in India and abroad? +
Yes, absolutely. All our classes are conducted live via Google Meet, so you can attend from anywhere — whether you're in Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Delhi, Mumbai, or any other city in India. You can also join from outside India (UAE, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, or any country with internet). Same professor, same syllabus, same quality instruction regardless of your location.
Who can join these Class 12 Physics and Chemistry classes? +
Students in Class 12 under CBSE or TN State Board can join. Classes are open to both complete beginners starting from scratch and advanced students looking to strengthen their concepts before exams. Whether you're studying in a school in Kanchipuram, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, or online from anywhere else, you can enroll.
What is the course duration for Class 12 coaching? +
The course runs continuously all the way through to your board exams, regardless of when you join. Once the full syllabus is covered, we move into structured revision, practice test series, mock exams, and personalized feedback to help you identify gaps and reach your target score. We recommend joining as early as possible to get the most comprehensive benefit.
What subjects are taught — Physics, Chemistry, or both? +
Both Physics and Chemistry for Class 12 are taught together in the same program. We cover both CBSE and TN State Board syllabi in detail. For Class 10 CBSE Science (all three subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology), we offer free video lessons on our YouTube channel (@CBSEdigest) with complete syllabus coverage, previous year papers, and chapter-wise notes.
How is the teaching approach different from other coaching centers? +
Prof. Dr. S. Balaji uses a concept-first teaching method — building strong fundamentals before moving to problem solving. We don't teach for entrance exams (NEET/JEE); we teach specifically for board exams, focusing on answer-writing format, value points, derivation clarity, and marking scheme awareness. Regular practice tests track each student's progress, and the small batch size ensures personalized attention for every student.
What does the course curriculum include? What topics are covered? +
We cover the complete CBSE NCERT or TN State Board syllabus chapter by chapter. For CBSE, this includes all chapters of Physics (thermodynamics, electromagnetism, optics, modern physics, etc.) and Chemistry (organic, inorganic, physical chemistry, coordination compounds, etc.). We also include practice with previous year papers (10+ years), full-length mock exams, problem-solving sessions, and derivation practice specific to board exam formats.
Are there practice tests and mock exams? How often? +
Yes, regular practice tests are core to the program. We conduct topic-wise tests, chapter-wise tests, and full-length board-pattern mock exams. As the board exams approach, the frequency of mock exams increases. Every test includes detailed feedback and marking scheme guidance so you understand exactly what the examiner is looking for.
Is this coaching suitable for both weak and advanced students? +
Yes, absolutely. The concept-first teaching method starts from fundamentals, so beginners and weak students build a solid base. Advanced students benefit equally from the structured board-pattern practice, marking scheme training, and competitive mock exams. Small batch sizes allow personalized attention for all levels.

What is CBSE Digest? Is it completely free? +
CBSE Digest is a 100% free, complete Class 10 CBSE Science lecture series on YouTube (@CBSEdigest). Every chapter is covered from basics through to board exam requirements. It includes concept explanations, solved previous year papers, one-shot revision videos, and chapter-wise notes. Everything a Class 10 student needs — completely free, no sign-up required.
Can I attend classes online? Can I switch between online and in-person? +
Yes, classes are conducted live via Google Meet — you can attend from anywhere in the world. We use professional-grade audio and video to make online classes feel just like being in the classroom. Call us to receive your access link and batch details before the session begins. Switching is completely flexible. If you've enrolled for in-person classes but can't make it on a particular day, you can simply join online — so you never miss a session.
What are the batches available? Are there flexible timing options? +
We offer two batches for CBSE students — a weekday batch (Mon-Sat, 5:30 AM - 6:30 AM) and a weekend batch (Sat 6:00 PM - 8:30 PM, Sun 6:00 AM - 8:30 AM) — so you can choose what fits your schedule. For TN State Board students, we currently run a weekday batch (Mon-Sat, 6:30 AM - 7:30 AM). All batches are available in classroom, online, and hybrid modes.
What if I join mid-way through the syllabus? Can I catch up? +
Yes, you can join at any point. Students who join mid-syllabus are accommodated and catch up through recorded sessions and focused revision. However, joining early is strongly recommended to get the full benefit of concept-building from the foundation before moving to complex topics and board-pattern practice.

Yes, dedicated doubt-clearing sessions are part of every batch. Students can raise doubts during these dedicated sessions and get one-to-one support. For ongoing questions between sessions, you can also reach out directly via WhatsApp or email (prof.balaji13@gmail.com).
