ABOUT ME

The mind behind the method.

25+ years of teaching, an IIT Madras postdoc, and 50+ international publications — all in service of one goal: your board exam success.

With a strong foundation in chemistry and a focus on electrochemistry and nanomaterials, I have cultivated a diverse academic and research career.

I hold a PhD in Physical Chemistry from the University of Madras (1999), following my Postdoctoral Fellowships at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras (1998) and Sunchon National University in South Korea (2005–2007), where I worked in the Department of Chemical Engineering.

My research further expanded during a visiting scientist tenure at Jawaharlal Nehru Centre for Advanced Scientific Research (JNCASR), Bengaluru, focusing on nanomaterials and nanoelectrocatalysis in 2010.

I was also honoured to be selected as a Brainpool Research Fellow at Sunchon National University from 2014 to 2015 — one of only 32 researchers worldwide chosen by the Government of South Korea for groundbreaking research in electrochemistry.

My academic journey began with a Bachelor of Science in Chemistry from the University of Madras (1986–1989), where I graduated first in my class, and continued with a Master of Science in Applied Chemistry from Anna University (1989–1991), earning first-class distinction.

FAQS

Got questions? We've got answers

Everything you need to know before booking your free demo class.

Who can join these classes? +
Students in Class 12 under CBSE or TN State Board can join. Classes are open to both beginners starting from scratch and students looking to revise and strengthen their concepts before exams.
What is the course duration? +
The course runs all the way through to your board exams, regardless of when you join. Once the full syllabus is covered, we move into structured revision and a practice test series — with personalised feedback to help each student identify gaps and reach their target score. That said, we recommend joining as early as possible to get the most out of the program.
What subjects are taught? +
Physics and Chemistry for Class 12 (both CBSE and TN State Board). For Class 10 CBSE Science, video lessons are available for free on our YouTube channel — covering concepts, formulas, diagrams, and NCERT questions with notes focusing on board exams.
How is the teaching approach different? +
Prof. Dr. S. Balaji uses a concept-first teaching method — building strong fundamentals before moving to problem solving. Regular practice tests are conducted to track each student's progress, and the small batch size ensures personalized attention.
Is there a free demo class? +
Yes! We offer a free demo class with absolutely no commitment required. Just Call or WhatsApp us before coming to the class in-person or to get the Google Meet invite to attend online.
How much is the fees? +
We'd love to share the fee details with you personally! Drop us a WhatsApp message or give us a call — we'll answer all your questions and help you find the right batch too.
What is CBSE Digest? Is it completely free? +
CBSE Digest is a complete Class 10 Science lecture series on YouTube — and yes, it is 100% free. Every chapter is covered from the basics all the way through to what you need for your board exams. The series includes concept explanations, theory, exam-focused lessons, and notes — everything a Class 10 student needs in one place.
Can I attend classes online? Can I switch between online and in-person? +
Yes, classes are conducted live via Google Meet — you can attend from anywhere in the world. We use professional-grade audio and video to make online classes feel just like being in the classroom. Call us to receive your access link and batch details before the session begins. Switching is completely flexible. If you've enrolled for in-person classes but can't make it on a particular day, you can simply join online — so you never miss a session.
What are the batches available? +
We offer two batches for CBSE students — a weekday batch and a weekend batch — so you can choose what fits your schedule. For TN State Board students, we currently run a weekday batch. For exact timings, refer to the Courses section above.
Where are the classroom sessions held? +
Classroom sessions are held in Kanchipuram. If you are nearby, you are welcome to attend in person. The exact address is available on Google Maps — find the link in the Contact section of this website.
