What is CBSE Digest? +

CBSE Digest is a free educational initiative created by Prof. Dr. S. Balaji to help CBSE Class 10 students understand Science in a simple, concept-first manner. The YouTube channel contains complete chapter-wise video lectures, revision content, handwritten notes, and study resources designed to help students prepare confidently for their board examinations.

Who is CBSE Digest for? +

CBSE Digest is designed primarily for CBSE Class 10 students preparing for their Science board examination. It is useful for students who want stronger conceptual understanding, additional practice outside school, effective revision before exams, or support with difficult topics.

Why was CBSE Digest created? +

Over the years, I have seen many students struggle with Science because they lack a strong conceptual foundation during their school education. CBSE Digest was created to bridge that gap by providing high-quality learning resources that are freely accessible to every student, regardless of their background.

What resources are available on CBSE Digest? +

CBSE Digest includes:

  • Complete chapter-wise video lectures organised into separate playlists.
  • One Shot revision videos for every chapter to help students revise quickly before exams.
  • Chapter-wise handwritten notes to simplify learning and revision.
  • Regular educational content focused on improving conceptual understanding and board exam preparation.
Is CBSE Digest completely free? +

Yes. Every lecture, playlist, revision video, and study resource available through CBSE Digest is completely free. There are no subscription charges or hidden fees, allowing every student to learn without financial barriers.

What is the CBSE Study Planner? +

The CBSE Study Planner is a free online planning tool that creates a personalised study schedule for students preparing for their board examinations. Based on the student's available study hours, target exam date, completed chapters, and preferred study routine, it automatically generates a structured plan showing what to study each day.

Students can download the generated study plan as a PDF, print it, place it on their study desk or wall, and simply follow the schedule throughout their preparation.

How does the Study Planner help students score higher marks? +

Many students spend valuable time deciding what to study each day. The Study Planner removes this uncertainty by creating a balanced schedule that covers the remaining syllabus, revision, and practice within the available time. By consistently following the personalised plan, students can prepare more efficiently, stay organised, and maximise their chances of achieving excellent board examination results.

Do I need to register or create an account to use the Study Planner? +

No. The Study Planner does not require any registration or login. Simply enter your study preferences, generate your personalised study plan instantly, and download it as a PDF for future reference.
