## Why Board-Exam Focused Coaching?

TN State Board exams have a specific answer-writing format — marks are awarded step by step for derivations, formulas, diagrams, and units. Coaching programs built for entrance exams (NEET/JEE) do not train students for this format. Students who only focus on MCQ-based entrance prep often lose marks in board exams on steps that are separately awarded.

This program trains for the TN State Board format from day one: structured answer writing, defined value points, and full practical/internal assessment coverage.
