## Why Board-Exam Focused Coaching?

CBSE board exams and JEE/NEET entrance exams test the same syllabus but with entirely different formats:

- **Board exams** use answer-writing with value points, award marks for each defined step, and include internal assessment / practical marks that entrance coaching entirely ignores.
- **Entrance exams** use MCQs with negative marking and test depth and speed over structured writing.

Students who train only for entrance exams often underperform in board exams because they never practice the specific format the board examiner uses to award marks. This program trains for the board format from day one.
