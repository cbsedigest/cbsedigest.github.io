## What is CBSE Digest?

CBSE Digest is a free YouTube series by Prof. Dr. S. Balaji covering the complete CBSE Class 10 Science syllabus. It is aimed at students preparing for the CBSE Class 10 board exams and targets scores of 95+.

This is a non-commercial, publicly available resource. No signup, no payment, no subscription required.

## CBSE Class 10 Science: Marks Distribution

- **Theory paper:** 80 marks
- **Internal assessment:** 20 marks (periodic tests, practicals, portfolio, subject enrichment)

Unit-wise weightage (approximate, based on CBSE pattern):
- Chemical Substances: 25 marks
- World of Living: 25 marks
- Natural Phenomena: 12 marks
- Effects of Current: 13 marks
- Natural Resources: 5 marks

## Study Roadmap for 90+ Score

1. Complete each chapter concept-by-concept before moving to problems
2. Solve NCERT in-text and exercise questions fully
3. Practice 10 years of PYQs chapter-wise
4. Use one-shot revision videos for full-chapter review before exams
5. Maintain an error log — track mistakes in practice tests and revisit weak areas
6. Prepare for practicals and internal assessment alongside theory
