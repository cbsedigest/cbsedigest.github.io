## Frequently Asked Questions

**Who can join?**
Open to Class 12 students under CBSE or TN State Board — both beginners and students seeking revision before exams.

**How long do courses run?**
Courses run until the board exams. Students may join at any point; the program adapts into revision and practice-test phases as needed. Early joining is recommended.

**What subjects are covered?**
Physics and Chemistry for Class 12 (CBSE and TN State Board). Class 10 CBSE Science is offered free via YouTube only — not a paid/live course.

**What is the teaching approach?**
Concept-first method building fundamentals before problem-solving. Regular practice tests track progress; small batch sizes for personalized attention.

**Is there a free demo?**
Yes — available for all courses, no commitment required. Booked via phone or WhatsApp.

**What are the fees?**
Fees are shared directly via phone or WhatsApp call. Not listed publicly on the website.

**Can I attend online from another city?**
Yes — students can attend fully online from anywhere via Google Meet.
